About

________

Mustle is a service for educators to develop content that meets the learning needs of their students. Using the old adage of "read it, write it, and say it", Mustle takes into account students natural affinity for answering questions in a visual, kinetic, or auditory format.

We learn from students to create a individualized learning paths and can provide analytics to improve the learning experience. Educators of all disciplines can easily create content for students that will automatically be tailored to their natural learning styles.
